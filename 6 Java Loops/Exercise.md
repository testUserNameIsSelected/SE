  negative and zeros entered

### Loop exercise 2 :

- Capture the numbers between 1000 and 10,000 that are equal to the sum of the first two digits and the last two digits.

#### Example :

* 1001
* 1010
* 1102
* 1111
* ....
* 9889
* 9898
* ....
* 9999
